You may use your Virtual Account only after it is activated and virtually delivered. A Visa debit Virtual Account number and an expiration date will be established for you. Value will be loaded in the Virtual Account by your Program Sponsor. Upon any purchase or other transaction, the amount available in the Virtual Account will be reduced by the amount of such withdrawal or purchase.

You are responsible for all authorized transactions using your Virtual Account. You do not have the right to stop payment on any purchase transaction originated by use of your Virtual Account.

Your virtual account may only be used for purchases consistent with this agreement. You may use your virtual account wherever visa is accepted, however the virtual account cannot be used for:

  • Any transaction that requires in person presentment of a physical debit or credit card;
  • "Dual mode" transactions (e.g., theatre ticket purchases, hotel stays, car rentals, online purchases picked up in person, and other transactions where subsequent presentment of a physical debit or credit card bearing the same account number as the virtual account used to purchase the item over the internet is required in order to obtain the item).
